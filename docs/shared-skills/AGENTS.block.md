## Shared Skills

- Treat this block as a routing shim: when a listed workflow applies, invoke the matching shared-skills skill and follow its `SKILL.md`.
- Use `requirements-packet` for ambiguous requirements and open questions; use `spec-contract`, `plan-contract`, and `spec-plan-coverage` when specs, plans, tests, implementation evidence, or completion claims need traceability.
- Use `research-plan`, `source-ledger`, and `claim-evidence-map` for source-backed research, evidence ledgers, claim confidence, and counterevidence review.
- Route all test-related work through `testing-workflow` first; it may dispatch to `scenario-test-designer`, `test-suite-reconciliation`, `test-plan-contract`, `tdd-cycle`, `test-adequacy-reviewer`, `test-reconciliation-reviewer`, or `verification-gate`.
- Use `implementation-discipline`, `debugging-discipline`, `comment-writing`, `implementation-evidence`, and `verification-gate` for scoped execution, investigation, comments, evidence, and before claiming work is complete.
- Keep detailed workflows in the installed shared-skills `SKILL.md` and `references/*`; use project-local rules when shared-skills is absent, and use the install/status diagnostic.
